Manure left on Pasture


Greenhouse Gases (GHG) emissions data from manure left on pasture consist of nitrous oxide gas from nitrogen additions to managed soils from grazing livestock. Computed at Tier 1 following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories; available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1961 to present, with annual updates, and projections for 2030 and 2050.
